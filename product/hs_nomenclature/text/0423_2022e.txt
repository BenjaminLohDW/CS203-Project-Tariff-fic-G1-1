 
 
Chapter 23 
 
Residues and waste from the food industries; 
prepared animal fodder 
 
Note. 
1.- Heading 23.09 includes products of a kind used in animal feeding, not elsewhere specified or 
included, obtained by processing vegetable or animal materials to such an extent that they have lost 
the essential characteristics of the original material, other than vegetable waste, vegetable residues and 
by-products of such processing. 
Subheading Note. 
1.- For the purposes of subheading 2306.41, the expression “low erucic acid rape or colza seeds” means 
seeds as defined in Subheading Note 1 to Chapter 12.
