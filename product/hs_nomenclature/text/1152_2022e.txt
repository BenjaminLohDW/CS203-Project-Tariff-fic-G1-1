 
 
Chapter 52 
 
Cotton 
 
Subheading Note. 
1.- For the purposes of subheadings 5209.42 and 5211.42, the expression “denim” means fabrics of yarns 
of different colours, of 3-thread or 4-thread twill, including broken twill, warp faced, the warp yarns 
of which are of one and the same colour and the weft yarns of which are unbleached, bleached, dyed 
grey or coloured a lighter shade of the colour of the warp yarns.
